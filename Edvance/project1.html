Edvance

Digital platform for a joint network of Italy's top universities






Edvance is a digital platform for higher education, built by a network of Italy’s top universities. The brand identity is rooted in the fusion of digital literacy and human brilliance, balancing technological innovation with the intellectual depth of academia.

My role spanned branding, UX, and UI design, shaping a visual and interactive language that reflects the platform’s mission: making advanced education accessible, intuitive, and engaging. A key aspect of the project was developing a custom image generator aligned with the art direction. Designed for ease of use, this tool enables even non-experts to create course cards and educational materials that maintain a cohesive visual identity—seamlessly integrating automation with design excellence.
