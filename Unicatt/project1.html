Università Cattolica

Lancio della facoltà di Scienze Matematiche, Fisiche e Informatiche






This out-of-home campaign for Università Cattolica’s Faculty of Mathematical Sciences speaks directly to the next generation of STEM students—those who see the world through logic, patterns, and hidden structures.

Divided into three subjects (Mathematics, Computer Science, and Physics), the campaign challenges the sharpest minds with a cryptic interplay of copy and visuals, unveiling the hidden wonders of each field. The visuals, reminiscent of technical drawings, embrace an aseptic aesthetic, stripping away the unnecessary to let pure logic and beauty emerge.

This campaign is not just an invitation to study—it’s a test of curiosity, intelligence, and the thrill of discovery.
